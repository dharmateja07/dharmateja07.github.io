About me

I'm Dharma Teja Lanka, a data analytics engineer in the making — currently completing my Master's at George Mason University in Fairfax, VA, where I've maintained a 3.5 GPA while diving deep into the intersection of data, machine learning, and real-world decision-making.

My journey started with a B.Tech in AI & Data Science in India, where I first got hooked on the idea that hidden in every dataset is a story worth telling. Since then I've built chatbots with NLP, created marketing dashboards with Google Analytics and Looker Studio, developed deep learning models for image captioning and speech emotion recognition, and shipped web applications that put data in front of the people who need it.

I'm equally comfortable working in Python, SQL, and R as I am presenting findings to stakeholders or documenting a data pipeline for a future team. I believe great analytics isn't just about the numbers — it's about making the right people care about them.

When I'm not building models or dashboards, I'm exploring how emerging AI tools are reshaping the way we work — and figuring out how to stay a step ahead.
